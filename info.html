About the Club

The Yorkville High School Computer Programming Club is a place for students passionate about coding, game design, and problem-solving. We aim to create an environment where everyone — from beginners to experienced programmers — can collaborate, learn, and build amazing projects together.

Meeting Details

  • Meeting Days: Every Wednesday during Flex
  • Time: 9:35AM - 10:15AM
  • Location: Room C125 (in the main building)
  • Sponsor: Mrs. Pickett

What We Do

From learning coding fundamentals to participating in game jams and competitions, the club focuses on creativity and hands-on learning. Our members work on group projects, explore new programming languages, and help one another develop technical and teamwork skills.
